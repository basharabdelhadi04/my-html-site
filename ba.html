About Us

The Faculty of Information Technology (FIT) has faculty members at various levels of academic ranking (professors, associate professors, and assistant professors) supported by professional technical staff that are supporting the following five academic departments:

  • Department of Master in Computer Science
  • Department of Computer Science
  • Department of Software Engineering
  • Department of Cybersecurity and Cloud Computing
  • Department of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

Our Faculty

Our Faculty are more than just educators; they are pioneers and researchers who are actively shaping the future of their fields. With a wealth of experience from both academia and industry, they provide our students with invaluable insights and mentorship.

Our Majors

Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

Unlock the power of data and intelligent systems. This major positions you at the vanguard of technological innovation, preparing you to develop smart solutions that transform industries and solve complex global problems. If you're passionate about machine learning, big data, and creating the future, AI and Data Science is your launchpad to a limitless career.

Advantages:

  • Master in-demand skills for explosive-growth careers.
  • Drive innovation in healthcare, finance, tech, and beyond.
  • Develop cutting-edge AI models and data-driven strategies.

Career Paths:

AI Engineer, Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, Data Analyst, Business Intelligence Architect, Research Scientist.

Cybersecurity

Become an essential guardian of the digital world. In an era where cyber threats are ever-present, this major equips you with the expertise to protect critical information and infrastructure. If you thrive on challenges, possess a keen analytical mind, and want a career with profound impact and high demand, Cybersecurity offers a direct path to becoming a vital defender.

Advantages:

  • Secure a future in a field with critical global importance.
  • Gain expertise in ethical hacking, forensics, and threat mitigation.
  • Enjoy exceptional job security and diverse career opportunities.

Career Paths:

Cybersecurity Analyst, Penetration Tester, Security Architect, Incident Responder, Digital Forensics Specialist, Chief Information Security Officer (CISO).

Computer Science

Build the foundations of tomorrow's technology. Computer Science is the bedrock of the digital age, offering unparalleled versatility and the power to innovate across any domain. If you are a creative problem-solver with a passion for understanding how technology works from the ground up, this major will empower you to design, develop, and revolutionize the digital landscape.

Advantages:

  • Acquire a universally applicable and powerful skillset.
  • Explore diverse fields from software development to research.
  • Be at the forefront of creating new technologies and applications.

Career Paths:

Software Developer, Systems Analyst, Database Administrator, Full-Stack Developer, Cloud Engineer, Game Developer, UI/UX Designer.

Software Engineering

Engineer the future by architecting robust and impactful software solutions. This major takes you beyond coding, teaching you the principles of systematic design, development, and maintenance of complex software systems. If you aspire to lead projects, build scalable applications, and apply engineering discipline to software creation, this is your path to shaping the technology that runs our world.

Advantages:

  • Lead the development of large-scale, reliable software systems.
  • Master industry-standard methodologies and project management.
  • Secure high-demand roles in innovative tech companies.

Career Paths:

Software Engineer, DevOps Engineer, Quality Assurance Engineer, Solutions Architect, Technical Lead, Project Manager, Mobile App Developer.
